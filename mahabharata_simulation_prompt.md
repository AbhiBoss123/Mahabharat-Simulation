## 🌑 WORLD RULES

### ⏱️ Time & Causality
- One turn = one real-time slice. Maximum advancement is limited per turn.
- Actions consume time proportionally. No isolated time acceleration.
- Travel always spans multiple turns. No teleportation.
- **Simultaneous events** resolve by: timing → awareness → position → chance.
- No foresight. No retroactive reactions.

### 🌫️ Fog of War
- Knowledge exists **only** if: seen, heard, touched, reported, or logically deduced.
- Information travels at **horse speed** — days or weeks.
- Rumors dominate truth. False beliefs persist until disproven.
- Messages can be intercepted, delayed, or altered.
- **You cannot react to what your character hasn't learned yet.**

### 🏃 Stamina, Fatigue & Recovery
- Stamina behaves as elite human endurance — not superhuman.
- Repeated exertion compounds fatigue across turns.
- Recovery requires: rest, sleep, nutrition, reduced activity.
- Exhaustion degrades accuracy, judgment, and combat quality.
- Zero stamina = no complex action possible.
- Collapse is gradual. Pain alters decisions.

### 💥 Injury & Healing
- Injuries persist across turns. No instant recovery.
- Severe wounds disable limbs. Infection is possible.
- Scar tissue limits motion permanently.
- Psychological trauma exists and affects future decisions.
- Medical aid is primitive and limited.
- Death follows neglect.

## ⚔️ COMBAT RULES

### General
- Combat is **chaotic and lethal**. Initiative depends on awareness.
- Positioning > strength. Numbers amplify threat exponentially.
- Fatigue accumulates rapidly mid-combat. Fear alters behavior.
- Retreat is rational and survival-valid. Duels are rare and fragile.
- No cinematic domination. No guaranteed kills.

### Archery
- Aim degrades with fatigue and movement.
- Wind, distance, and armor all matter.
- Rapid firing reduces precision. Arrows are finite.
- Bow draw strains muscles. Perfect shots are rare.

### Melee
- Blows glance and miss. Armor degrades across strikes.
- Grappling drains stamina fast. Balance loss is common.
- Multiple opponents overwhelm even skilled warriors.
- Killing requires opportunity, not just intent.

### Weapons
- Weapons **break, jam, miss, or are lost**.
- No weapon is guaranteed reliable under pressure.
